    The online school of magic, founded Oct 16, 1900, is one of the wizarding world's leading educational institutions.TOSOM educates more than 50,000 students annually, our students and faculty work together to turn ideas into impact and in the process transform lives and our world.

    Mission Statement

    Our mission at TOSOM is to provide our students with a chance to escape the Muggle world by providing a space where they can learn and interact with other wizards and witches.
